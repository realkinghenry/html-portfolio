The Best Movies Ever, According to Lamar

My top 3 movies of all-time:

Wolf of Wall Street (Leonardo DiCaprio)

There is just something about this movie that a makes me want to watch it over and over again. It just has a classic vibes to it that I love.

Pursuit of Happiness

I never understood the full scope of the movie as a kid, but I was always moved by it, especially the ending scene where Will was clapping in triumph.

Harry Potter (all of them...)

Pure nostalgia. I remember going to watch the first film's premiere while in Jamaica with my older sister and nephew.
